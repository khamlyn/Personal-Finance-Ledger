How to Export from Your Bank
Most banks offer CSV export under Account Activity → Download → CSV. Common bank paths:
· TD / RBC / Scotiabank — Accounts → Download transactions → CSV
· BMO — My Accounts → Export
· Chase / BoA / Wells Fargo — Activity → Download → CSV

Your CSV should have columns for Date, Description, Amount (at minimum). Category is optional.
